Blackgold Kdenterprise Launches BGKD Crypto Token

Maritime Industry Leader Introduces Blockchain-Based Digital Asset

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE | January 23, 2025

Middletown, DE — Blackgold Kdenterprise, a leading maritime and offshore operations company established in 1995, is proud to announce the upcoming launch of the BGKD Crypto Token on June 1, 2025.

The BGKD token represents a groundbreaking fusion of traditional maritime excellence and cutting-edge blockchain technology. This digital asset is designed to power the maritime and energy frontier, backed by decades of innovation in offshore operations, vessel management, and subsea gold exploration.

Token Overview

Total Supply: 100,000,000 BGKD
Symbol: BGKD
Launch Date: June 1, 2025
Utility: Maritime technology platforms, logistics operations, and investment transparency

Blackgold Kdenterprise brings unparalleled maritime expertise to the blockchain space, operating a fleet of over 200 vessels with more than 5,000 crew members across 45 countries. With 30 years of industry leadership, the company offers comprehensive services including:

  • Ship Brokerage and Technical Management
  • Crew Management and Offshore Crewing
  • Underwater Mining with Advanced ROV Technology
  • Vessel Management and Ship Repairs
  • Oil & Gas Sector Operations
